# The Modern AI PM in the Age of Agents

## Summary
As agents turn specifications into prototypes, the PM role shifts away from translating between business and engineering and toward shaping intent, curating context, judging output, and deciding what is worth building.

## Key Takeaways
- The spec is becoming closer to the product because agents can act directly on it.
- PMs can prototype with agents before handing work to engineers for hardening.
- The bottleneck moves upstream: choosing the right problem, framing it well, and evaluating taste.
- Context curation becomes a PM superpower.
- Ambiguity should often be explored with agents before being collapsed into requirements.
- Engineers still matter for architecture, reliability, maintainability, and production ownership.
