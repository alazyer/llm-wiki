# Paperclip AI Company Orchestration

## Summary
Paperclip frames multi-agent work as an organization: roles, goals, budgets, tickets, heartbeats, governance, and audit trails. It does not replace individual agents; it coordinates them into an operating system for agent teams.

## Key Takeaways
- Org charts and roles make agent responsibilities visible.
- Goals and budgets prevent unlimited work under vague instructions.
- Tickets give humans an audit trail of what happened and why.
- Heartbeats schedule work without constantly spending tokens.
- Governance and versioning keep humans in control of the organization.
- This pattern is most useful once someone is already running multiple agents.
